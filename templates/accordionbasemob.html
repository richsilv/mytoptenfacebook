To get started, enter the title and artist of one of your favourite artists and hit the tick on the right hand side.

Now select one of the available providers to search their database for your track.

The drop-down menu contains all the available matches. Choose the best fit!

Finally, tweak the title and artist so that they're as you want them to appear, and enter a reason why you love your chosen track. Hit the tick, and move on to the next song!

Now that you have at least one song entered, you can save your list at any time by hitting the Save button, and the same set of songs will be there next time you login. Once you've chosen ten, you'll be able to hit Confirm, and you'll be a fully-fledged My Top Ten User!
